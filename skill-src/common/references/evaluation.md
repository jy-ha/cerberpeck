# Evaluation

- Run every critique in a fresh independent session.
- Keep reviewers read-only and show only their review bundle.
- Blind A/B labels and hide version age, implementation intent, and other reviews.
- Accept 1–5 scores in 0.5 increments, but decide from evidence, contract fit, regressions, and preference rather than score totals alone.
- Reject a Challenger with failed objective gates.
- Do not stop after the first rejected Challenger.
